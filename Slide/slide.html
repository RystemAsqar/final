purpose

The mission of “PREESS”, LLC -- to help people see the world, be sure to find an acceptable option for any tourist, to change the middle class opinion that going abroad is not affordable, through professional advice and pleasant discounts. Attract clients with an individual approach, professionalism and exciting atmosphere. A travel agency that ignores the interests of its customers and has no strategy for the future risks losing its place in the tourism market. In order to create a competitive enterprise, it is necessary not only to modernize production and management, but also to know clearly what it is done for, what goal should be achieved. And the main thing should be the ability to identify and then quickly and effectively use its advantages in competition. All efforts of the enterprise should be directed to the development of those qualities, which favorably distinguish it from potential or real competitors.
